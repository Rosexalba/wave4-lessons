Movie highlights

Monster House

Monster House is an animated horror-comedy film directed by Gil Kenan and produced by Robert Zemeckis and Steven Spielberg. The story follows three kids—DJ, Chowder, and Jenny—who discover that a creepy old house in their neighborhood is actually alive and devours anything that comes near it. As Halloween approaches, the trio must uncover the house’s dark secret and stop it before it endangers the whole town. The film blends spooky elements with humor and heart, making it a fun, family-friendly thriller. 2006

Halloweentown

Halloweentown is a Disney Channel original movie released in 1998. It follows Marnie Piper, a teenage girl who discovers she's a witch and belongs to a secret magical realm called Halloweentown—a place where supernatural creatures like ghosts, goblins, and witches live openly. When dark forces threaten the town, Marnie and her family must embrace their powers to save it. The film blends lighthearted fantasy, family themes, and Halloween charm, and has become a cult favorite during the spooky season. 1998
